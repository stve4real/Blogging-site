Setting up a poultary farm

Poultry farming is a type of animal husbandry that involves raising domesticated birds for their meat, eggs, feathers, and manure. Some common birds raised for poultry farming include chickens, turkeys, ducks, and geese.

Here are some things to consider when starting a poultry farm:
